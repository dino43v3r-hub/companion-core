# Companion Core MVP Testing

This test is for people trying the first version of Companion Core.

The goal is to see if the first conversation feels warm, simple, and easy to follow.

## What To Click First

1. Open the Companion Core page.
2. Look at the welcome screen.
3. Click **Begin gently**.

## What To Type

When the guide asks what you have enjoyed recently, type a simple answer.

You might write:

- "I enjoyed sitting outside this morning."
- "I had coffee with my daughter."
- "I listened to some music I like."
- "I cooked dinner and it turned out well."

There are no right or wrong answers.

Keep typing when the guide asks another gentle question.

## What To Notice

As you try it, notice:

- Is the text easy to read?
- Do the buttons feel clear?
- Does the first question feel friendly?
- Does it feel like a conversation?
- Do you know what to do next?
- Does the profile at the end feel kind and respectful?
- Does the profile avoid putting you in a box?

## Questions To Ask After Trying It

- What did you think this page was for?
- Was anything hard to read?
- Was anything hard to click?
- Did any question feel too personal?
- Did any reply feel fake, cold, or odd?
- Did the profile sound like it was listening?
- Did the profile say anything that felt wrong?
- Would you want to keep the profile?
- What would make this feel more comfortable?

## Signs The Experience Is Working

The experience is probably working if:

- The person knows to click **Begin gently**.
- The person understands they can type in their own words.
- The person feels free to write a short answer.
- The guide feels warm and patient.
- The person wants to keep going for a few replies.
- The final profile feels gentle and not too personal.
- The person understands they can save a copy.

## Signs It Feels Confusing Or Too Technical

The experience may need work if:

- The person is not sure what to click.
- The person asks what they are supposed to type.
- The page feels like a form or test.
- The words feel too fancy or unclear.
- The guide sounds like a robot.
- The profile feels like a label or judgment.
- The person worries that the app knows too much.
- The download button is confusing.
- The text is too small or crowded.
