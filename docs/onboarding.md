# Onboarding

The onboarding experience begins with a welcome screen and a clear Begin button.

The conversation starts with:

> "I'm glad you're here. What's something you've enjoyed recently?"

The app should not feel like a form, a quiz, or a medical intake.

The app should:

- Use plain language
- Ask one warm thing at a time
- Let the person answer freely
- Reply with care
- Notice tone, detail, and interests
- Build a small first profile after several exchanges

The app should avoid:

- Direct personality questions
- Labels
- Scores
- Diagnosis language
- Technical language
- Pressure to answer a certain way
