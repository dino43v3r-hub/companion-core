# Welcome And Handoff

The experience begins with a welcome screen and a clear conversation button.

The conversation starts with:

> "What would you like me to call you?"

After the person shares a name, the guide uses it naturally and asks:

> "Thanks, [name]. Let's spend a few minutes talking. What's something you've enjoyed recently?"

The app should not feel like a form, a quiz, or a medical intake.

The app should:

- Use plain language
- Ask one warm thing at a time
- Let the person answer freely
- Reply with care
- Notice meaningful patterns in the person's answers
- Ask what the companion should be called
- Ask whether the companion should have a form or appearance
- Build a portable Companion Package after several exchanges
- Help the person take the package to ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, Gemini, or another AI platform

The app should avoid:

- Direct personality questions
- Labels
- Scores
- Diagnosis language
- Technical language
- Pressure to answer a certain way
- Ending at download without clear next steps
